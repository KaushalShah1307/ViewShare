Believe, Trust & Love

Posted by Kaushal Shah on August 24, 2012

I know it`s not going to be easy for you when I am gone,
Life won`t be the same for you when I am gone,
You`ll weep; feel remorseful, disheartened, won`t be easy when I am gone
Within you`ll cry, feel lonely, recalling the days before I had gone.

But it`s not YOU, it`s US, and will always be the same,
No matter wherever I am, I go, will always be the same,
Believe me I won`t change, everything will always be the same,
I`ll miss you every second of my life, my love for you will always be the same.

I want you to be mine, and the same as you are,
Can`t bear to see u sad, please be happy the same as you are,
Want are future to be as cheerful, the same as you are,
I promise, will be the same and won`t ever forget, who you are....!!
