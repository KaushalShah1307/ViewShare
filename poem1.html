Life, What I Believe

Posted by Kaushal Shah on September 24, 2014

Life isn`t what we think it is, its mere illusion,
It`s always about what we desire, our aims and the most vital…. our vision,
Failed, Struggled then succeeded, did everything to carve from others expectations But do we really believe in what we achieved or keep faking those untrue expressions

I have always heard elder saying "Either Do, or Die"
But the question that always troubles me and my conscious is the big " Y….????”
Why should I believe in what they say or ask me to do, achieve and become
Have deduced why they do so, cuz they couldn’t, they couldn’t reach there to overcome

A person hears joyous claps, jealous whispers when rewarded for his work and deeds
Perks, gifts, comforts, all is served in one plate to fulfill his desire, his needs
Father with chest inflated, mother with tears of joy, friends delighted and no one to impede
Perfect as one wants his life to be, the question is has he really done what he had dreamt to succeed

I don’t wanna criticize or question god’s art and his creation,
But I ask myself and others, why live just to leave a good impression,
Instead y not to believe in oneself and work towards your aim and avoid later regression
Cuz it’s just u and your soul and no one else who needs answers and reasons to mention

I believe in this and would like to state again,
Life isn't what we think it is, its mere illusion…….
