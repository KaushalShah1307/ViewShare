A Thought

Posted by Kaushal Shah on July 8, 2011

Lost In my thoughts, thinking here I sit
Desire to carve those beautiful memories, in words that fit
Right is the time, the moment and the episodes of memory are bittersweet
But still searching the start, the words I need are yet to click

Bag full of old memories, both happy and sad
Where to start is the question, is it with Mom or with Dad
Is it when I was born or should be the one, when I first time ran
All are mere phases of life, but beneath lies Gods dreams, his creative plan

Smile on my face, eyes filled with joyous tears is what I have
No words to pen for what you gave or from what I have
Want to say, want to write, the feeling within my heart I save
In your debt I am, lifelong, for the love and culture you gave

Lost in the thoughts, thinking here I sit
Desire to carve those beautiful memories, in words that fit……
