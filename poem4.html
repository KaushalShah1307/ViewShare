Reminiscence

Posted by Kaushal Shah on September 24, 2014

Can`t explain or narrate what you mean to me,
Yea can take efforts to describe in words what I see,
Giving away, doing for others, this is what you always try,
Can`t hurt nobody, nor make them cry,
But inside you are very sad, sad like a baby within you cry.

There you weep; here I feel the warmth of your tears,
It`s been weeks, but I feel already it`s been years.
Have got so much used to you, can`t stay put up with my peers,
Looking myself, I feel there`s nothing without you even in the mirrors.

Recalling you, your love, your madness, makes me nostalgic,
Was very young before to understand what is love and its logic,
Today looking at what I have achieved with you,
Makes me feel proud, delighted and li`l amazed as for me god`s finest magic lies in you.

Swear to god, will always love and care for you,
Will be back soon, to love you the way I used to do,
These two years, away from you, are not going to be easier,
But I know you`ll be around, taking care, to keep me happier.
